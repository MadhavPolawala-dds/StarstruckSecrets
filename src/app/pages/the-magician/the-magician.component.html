THE magician
LOVE
Be aware of recent love and relationships in your, just don't skip the trust and faithful part of your relationship, otherwise, you'll be regretting your own choice.
Don't live in illusions, anyone can fake themselves to use others, make sure that you are totally familiar with your new love.
Low willpower and an absence of firmness can give rise to insecurities in your fresh relationship. All these insecurities take place when chances of love and romance take place in your relationship. So make yourself more alert and show some excitement in yourself.
Finance
Your financial conditions may not be in your hand, and the reason for this is that you're wasting your resources and mastery. Boost up your assertiveness and get ready to show your most awaited side which is full of knowledge and experience.
The destitution of boldness can kill your willpower which will not let your financial gain increase. Try to transform your prowess and talent into something useful which will also increase your income or more money-making.
Career
The Magician reverse card reading complaint about not showing your ingenuity and not taking advantage of your opportunities properly.
Are you sure that all your efficiencies are manoeuvered correctly and in the right way? Ignoring the best is not as lucrative as you are not using your full potential to achieve something.
Real risks are not ignoring the facts but risks are something you have to take in your career if you want a bright future. Try to understand more about your work as clever people are always watching with hidden agendas.
