River
The arrow suggests that you must be cautious in your daily life. Determine the right orientation of the arrow in the coffee cup.
If the arrow points downwards, conditions may appear tough.
If indeed the arrow is pointed upwards, your life will be progressive.
Whereas if the arrow is horizontal, it indicates that your thoughts are mostly about something unpleasant.
